About Me

Hello, my name is Coral Darling and my pronouns are she/her. I am currently attending a full-stack software development bootcamp through the University of Oregon. I am also attending Portland State University as a senior, finishing up my BS in Art History and Japanese Language. I presently hold a Customer Support Specialist Tier I role at a Cybersecurities company, ZeroFox.

My focus and ultimate goal is to gain software development knowledge, and experience with which I can continue to help others within the scope of my career.
